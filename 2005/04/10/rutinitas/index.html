================================== Modifications to the SPAM Filtering ==================================
I made some modifications to the soam filtering system. While I am still
processing mail through SpamAssassin, I am no longer going to automatically
put the email into your ~/mail/spam folder. If you want to maintain this
functionality, please run "filter-spam" and the functionality will be
added to your account. You will be responsible for cleaning that spam
mail box (located at ~/mail/spam unless you modify things). The spam
box does count towards your disk quota.

The mail in your ~/mail/spam mail box can be checked via Pine, Mutt or any
email client (not POP) including IMAP and the web mail (you will probably
need to "subscribe" the it in order to see it in web mail).
